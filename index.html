Scope. Rates combine published, derived, and modeled values. Replace defaults with current quotes before making a commitment. Carbon is a comparative active use proxy based on average grid intensity and GPU nameplate power. It excludes PUE, other system power, embodied emissions, and marginal emissions. Published FLOPS describe hardware, not workload throughput. This utility is not a procurement quote or an emissions inventory.

The basis for each rate appears in the tooltip and CSV export. A small amber diamond marks modeled or unverified values. Published and derived values may still require region and configuration checks.
